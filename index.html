Site Plan Document - Individual Project

1. Site Name

Ciseaux Galants

Proposed Domain: ciseauxgalants.com

Name Justification:

"Ciseaux Galants" is French for "Elegant Scissors," which perfectly represents the salon's commitment to sophisticated, high-quality hair styling services. The name combines:

  • Ciseaux (Scissors): Represents the craft, precision, and artistry of hair cutting
  • Galants (Elegant/Gallant): Conveys sophistication, style, and premium service

The name suggests a European-inspired salon that focuses on elegance and professional craftsmanship, appealing to clients seeking premium hair services.

Domain Availability:

ciseauxgalants.com is available for registration. Alternative: ciseaux-galants.com

2. Site Purpose

The Ciseaux Galants website will serve as the primary digital platform for the salon, with the following purposes:

  • Online Booking System: Allow clients to schedule appointments 24/7 with real-time availability
  • Service Portfolio: Showcase all hair services with detailed descriptions and pricing
  • Stylist Introduction: Present the professional team with their specialties and experience
  • Client Information Hub: Provide location, hours, contact details, and parking information
  • Inspiration Gallery: Display before/after photos and style ideas
  • Product Sales: Offer professional hair care products for purchase
  • Promotional Platform: Share special offers, events, and new services

Target Audience:

  • Men and women aged 18-65 seeking premium hair services
  • Professionals looking for convenient online booking
  • Bridal parties and special event styling clients
  • Local residents and tourists in the area
  • Clients interested in European-style hair techniques

3. Scenarios

Common questions from potential website visitors:

Scenario 1: New Client Looking for Specific Service

Question: "Do you offer balayage hair coloring, and how much does it cost? Can I see examples of your work?"

Website Answer: Dedicated service pages with detailed descriptions, pricing, and before/after galleries. Online booking with service selection.

Scenario 2: Busy Professional Needing Appointment

Question: "What are your Saturday hours? Can I book a haircut for this weekend online?"

Website Answer: Clear business hours display and real-time online booking system with calendar integration showing available slots.

Scenario 3: Wedding Party Coordination

Question: "Do you offer bridal party packages? Can we schedule a trial before the wedding day?"

Website Answer: Special events section with package options, pricing, and dedicated booking for trials and group appointments.

Scenario 4: Product Inquiry

Question: "What professional shampoo do you recommend for color-treated hair? Can I purchase it online?"

Website Answer: Product catalog with detailed information, recommendations by hair type, and e-commerce functionality.
